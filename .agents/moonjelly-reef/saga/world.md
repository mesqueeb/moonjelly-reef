A warm reef shelf near a volcanic ridge, with bright tidepools above and deeper, darker channels below.

The workbench lives in a hollowed coral outcrop called the Lantern Nook, where tide, current, and gossip all arrive at once.

- Walrus, who steps back to see the whole reef and keeps the group honest about what matters
